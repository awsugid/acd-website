# Welcome to the New AWS User Group Indonesia Portal!

We are thrilled to announce the launch of our new website, designed to be the central hub for all things AWS in Indonesia.

## What's New?

- **Interactive Map**: Find user groups near you with our new interactive map.
- **Community Day Updates**: Stay tuned for the latest news on AWS Community Day Indonesia 2025.
- **Blog**: Read articles, tutorials, and announcements from the community (like this one!).

Whether you are a seasoned cloud architect or just starting your journey, there is a place for you in our community. 

- [Jakarta User Group](https://jakarta.awscommunity.id)
